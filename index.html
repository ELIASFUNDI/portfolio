About Me

I am a Geomatics Engineer and GIS Specialist with expertise in spatial analysis, cartographic design, remote sensing, and web-based geospatial systems.

Born on August 15, 2002, I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Geomatics Engineering and Geospatial Information Systems from Dedan Kimathi University of Technology. My passion lies in leveraging cutting-edge geospatial technologies to solve real-world problems in infrastructure planning, environmental monitoring, and sustainable development.

Throughout my academic journey and professional experiences, I have developed proficiency in GIS software (ArcGIS Pro, QGIS), spatial databases (PostgreSQL/PostGIS), web development (React, FastAPI), remote sensing analysis, and survey equipment operation. I have worked on diverse projects ranging from cartographic design for county governments to AI-powered spatial analysis systems.

I am available for freelance projects, employment opportunities, and further studies (MSc/PhD). My goal is to continue growing as a geospatial professional while contributing innovative solutions that address critical challenges in resource management and spatial planning.
